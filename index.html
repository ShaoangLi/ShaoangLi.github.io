About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at Stony Brook University, working with Prof. Jian Li. I received my Ph.D. from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Xiang-Yang Li and mentored by Prof. Lan Zhang. My research focuses on learning theory and sequential decision-making, with applications to large language models.
